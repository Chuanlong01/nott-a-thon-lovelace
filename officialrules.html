Objectives


1) To provide a platform for students to inter-connect, collaborate, and expand their network within the global field of technology.

2) Expose participants to a new environment to enhance their coding skills.

3)A vast majority of problems appearing in programming contests are mathematical or logical in nature.

4) To encourage healthy competition among participants while challenging them to utilise critical and creative thinking.

5) To strengthen the participants’ soft skills such as teamwork, communication, as well as time and pressure management.

6) To enhance the students’ participation in activities outside of their studies which develops their personal growth
