About

Nott-A-Thon: Lovelace is a programming competition organized by the Institution of Engineering and Technology UNM Student Chapter. Participants are challenged to solve problems with different levels of difficulties using computer algorithms, in a group of 3. The previous iteration of the event was successfully hosted back in January 2021. Participants from 14 universities located in 5 different countries: Malaysia, Indonesia, Japan, United Kingdom and France, with a total of 111 participants.
