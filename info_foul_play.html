Fouls and Misconduct

  1. Do NOT attempt to sabotage / hinder/ suppress / handicap other groups in any way.
  2. Do NOT share the competition link out to non-participants.
  3. DO report any configuration/platform/setup issues you encounter to the organizers.
  4. Actions that go against the sportsmanship of the competition such as match-fixing/bribery/protests are strictly forbidden.
