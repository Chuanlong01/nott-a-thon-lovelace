Competitive Programming


The aim of competitive programming is to sharpen problem solving skills in programming.
By limiting the competition time frame, participants learn to write efficient codes and algorithms to solve problems quickly.
A vast majority of problems appearing in programming contests are mathematical or logical in nature.

Nott-A-Thon: Lovelace


Nott-A-Thon:Lovelace is a competitive programming competition designed for programmers from different background with a mixture of different level of challenges.
