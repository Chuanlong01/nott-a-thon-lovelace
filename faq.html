FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Which platform will the competition be hosted on?

Hackerrank.com

Am I allowed to use any programming language?

The competition is limited to a few programming languages such as Python, Java, C, C++, and Kotlin.

I have never used Hackerrank before, can I still join?

Yes you can! We have prepared a section where you can practice and familiarise yourself with the platform’s environment. You can find it under home page > Mock Contest.

Must I be online throughout the 2 hours of the competition?

You don’t have to. You are given 2 hours to complete the challenges, however if you manage to solve it earlier you may submit and leave.

Can I sign up solo, or with 2 members instead of 3?

Yes, you may participate without any teammate, or as a team of 3 or less.

Can I sign up as a solo, but have a team of 3 during the competition?

No. You have to declare all the members of your team during registration.

How will I get my prize?

We will contact you and transfer the money to you (team leader) via bank transfer. So make sure you get a reliable group leader

Will there be a pre-competition briefing session before the competition starts?

No. We will send out emails to you with a brief introduction of what you should prepare before the competition starts, and please create a hackerank account before the competition.

How many Hackerrank account(s) do we need??

During competition, each team is required to use ONLY ONE account for submission. However, feel free to use your own account for mock contest.
